Our Amazing Animal Residents

Journey through diverse habitats and meet over 500 remarkable animals from around the globe. From the African Savanna to the Amazon Rainforest, each exhibit is carefully designed to replicate natural environments while providing exceptional care for our residents. Every animal has a story, and we're honored to share them with you.

African Lions

Meet our pride of majestic African lions, including Simba and Nala. Watch them lounge in the sun during feeding time at 2 PM daily.

Visitor Amenities & Services

We're dedicated to making your visit comfortable, accessible, and memorable. From dining to educational experiences, explore everything Riverside Wildlife Park has to offer. Your adventure awaits with world-class facilities and services designed for every member of your family.

Premium Membership

Unlimited annual visits, exclusive member events, early park access, and 20% discount at restaurants and gift shops. Join our conservation family today.

Dining Options

Three full-service restaurants, snack bars, and picnic areas. Enjoy locally-sourced meals with vegetarian, vegan, and allergy-friendly options available.

Educational Programs

Daily keeper talks, behind-the-scenes tours, summer camps, and school field trips. Learning opportunities for all ages year-round.

Free WiFi

Stay connected throughout the park. Download our mobile app for interactive maps, feeding schedules, and real-time animal activity updates.

Full Accessibility

Wheelchair rentals, accessible pathways, sensory-friendly hours, and accommodations for guests with disabilities. Everyone is welcome here.

Photo Opportunities

Designated photography spots, professional photo services, and up-close animal encounters. Capture memories that last a lifetime.

Mobile App

Navigate easily with our free app featuring GPS-enabled maps, augmented reality experiences, and push notifications for feeding times and special events.

Free Parking

Ample free parking for cars, RVs, and buses. Electric vehicle charging stations available. Convenient drop-off zones for guests with mobility needs.

Gift Shop

Sustainably-sourced merchandise, educational toys, plush animals, and unique souvenirs. All proceeds support our conservation programs.

Kids' Zone

Interactive play areas, splash pad, petting zoo, and playgrounds designed for different age groups. Family restrooms with nursing stations available.

Group Rates

Special pricing for groups of 15 or more, corporate events, and birthday parties. Private tour options available with advance booking.

Eco-Friendly

100% renewable energy powered, zero-waste initiatives, water conservation systems, and sustainable practices throughout. We walk the conservation talk.

Get in Touch

Have questions about visiting, memberships, educational programs, or events? We'd love to hear from you! Our guest services team typically responds within 24 hours.
