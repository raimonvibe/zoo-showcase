## Download & Resize Instructions

### Step 1: Download Images
1. Click on the image you want
2. Click the download button (usually top right)
3. Select the largest size available
4. Save to your computer

### Step 2: Create Gallery Thumbnails
For each gallery image, you need TWO versions:

**Full Size:**
- Location: `images/gallery/fulls/`
- Size: 1200x800px minimum
- Keep original aspect ratio

**Thumbnail:**
- Location: `images/gallery/thumbs/`
- Size: 400x300px
- Crop to fit if needed

### Recommended Free Image Editors
- **Online:**
  - Photopea (https://photopea.com) - Free Photoshop alternative
  - Canva (https://canva.com) - Easy resize tool
  - Pixlr (https://pixlr.com) - Quick editing

- **Desktop:**
  - GIMP (Free, cross-platform)
  - Paint.NET (Windows)
  - Preview (Mac - built-in)

## Quick Workflow

1. **Open this guide**
2. **Click the direct search links** for each image you need
3. **Browse and select** high-quality images
4. **Download** in the largest size available
5. **Rename** files according to the structure (01.jpg, 02.jpg, etc.)
6. **Resize** if necessary
7. **Place** in the correct folders
8. **Test** your website

## Alternative Search Strategy

If you can't find the perfect image with the suggested keywords, try:

1. **Broader terms:** "wild animal" instead of specific species
2. **Related terms:** "big cat" instead of "tiger"
3. **Activity-based:** "animal eating" "animal playing"
4. **Habitat-based:** "jungle cat" "savanna wildlife"
